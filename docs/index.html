Letter perception study

Hello,

Thank you for your interest in our study which is a research project undertaken by Mary Dyson and David Březina of Design Regression. The aim of the study is to investigate the effect of holistic processing within letter perception.

Your task

There are three parts:

  1. Short questionnaire
  2. Practice: tell the two letters look the same
  3. Main: tell if top halfs look the same

Statement of consent

You have to be 18 years or older to participate. All data will remain anonymous.

By participating in the study and returning your answers you are confirming that:

  1. You are 18 years or older.
  2. You have read the above information.
  3. You give your consent for the responses you provide to be used in our research.

If you wish to know more about the research or have a query about this study, please contact:

Mary Dyson, David Březina

Questionnaire

Please answer the following questions.

Would you consider yourself a fluent English speaker? Are you a designer?

Practice
